9 Features Pet Care Owners Actually Use (And Most Websites Skip)

Every feature on this list exists because we've seen it move the needle on booked leads. Nothing here is decoration.

No Transparent Pricing on Common Services

Bath, full groom, breed-specific grooming, nail trim, teeth brushing — pet owners shop these. Sites hiding prices behind 'Call for quote' lose to groomers publishing starting-at ranges per breed.

Online booking trapped in PetExec or Gingr iframe

Most pet care sites embed PetExec, Gingr, ProPet as a 600px iframe that breaks mobile and feels like a separate site. Pet owners abandon the booking flow when the experience suddenly switches platforms.

No groomer or trainer profiles with photos

Pet parents want to know who's handling their dog. First names, years of experience, certifications (NCMG, ABKA, IPDTA), specialty breeds — trust signals that shortcut anxious-first-time-customer hesitation.

Generic services page where breed-specific pages should live

Golden retriever grooming, poodle grooming, doodle de-matting — each is a separate search intent with different time and pricing. One services page ranks for none.

No daycare or boarding photos visible

Pet parents leaving dogs overnight want to see the kennel, play areas, sleeping setup. Sites without facility photos lose to facilities that show them.

Vaccination requirements not surfaced

DHPP, rabies, bordetella, canine influenza — required for daycare and boarding. Hidden requirements turn into front-desk frustration and lost first-visit conversions.

No recurring grooming plan visibility

Monthly grooming, breed-appropriate schedules (every 6 weeks for poodles, every 4 for doodles), recurring discounts — recurring revenue most pet care sites leave on the table.

No mobile grooming distinction where applicable

Mobile groomers and brick-and-mortar groomers serve different buyer mindsets. Mobile lacks visibility on most pet care sites that try to do both without splitting messaging.

Aggressive-dog and special-needs policies hidden

Owners of reactive dogs or seniors with conditions need to know if you accept them. Sites silent on these policies lose to facilities that publish them.

8 specializations, one playbook

Website design built around your specific pet care businesses niche

A dog grooming salon and an overnight boarding facility serve entirely different services to entirely different pet parents in entirely different decision states. We design for the niche.

1

Dog Grooming Salon

Breed-specific pricing, groomer profiles with certs, before-and-after gallery, recurring grooming plans, hand-stripping and specialty grooming.

2

Dog daycare facility

Vaccination requirements visible, play group structure, facility photos, half-day vs full-day pricing, pickup window flexibility.

Boarding facility

Overnight kennel photos, climate-controlled spaces, holiday pricing, owner update photos, multi-pet discounts.

4

Dog trainer

Certifications (CPDT-KA, KPA, IAABC, AKC CGC evaluator), service tiers (private, group, board-and-train), behavior-vs-obedience split, force-free methods.

Dog walker / pet sitter

Service area map, visit length options (20/30/60 min), insurance and bonding info, sitter profiles with photos.

Mobile grooming

Service area map, in-home pricing transparency, what's in the mobile unit, parking and water access requirements.

7

Cat grooming / care

Cat-only or cat-aware grooming, sedation-free positioning, mobile cat grooming, vet-tech-certified groomers.

8

Exotic & small pet care

Birds, reptiles, rabbits, ferrets — species expertise, specialized boarding setups, veterinary partnership for emergencies.

Frequently asked questions

Is the $999 price really all-in?

Yes. Logo, branding, pet care website, SEO content, on-page SEO setup, GBP optimization, and 30 days of post-launch support. Pet care software subscriptions are your existing tools.

Will my business rank #1 for 'dog groomer [my city]'?

No one can guarantee a #1 ranking, but with the right local SEO strategy, strong Google Business Profile optimization, quality content, reviews, and consistent marketing, we can significantly improve your chances of ranking higher for “dog groomer [your city]” and attracting more local customers.

Do you integrate with PetExec, Gingr, ProPet, Time to Pet?

Yes, we can work alongside PetExec, Gingr, ProPet, and Time To Pet. We don’t replace your existing pet care software — we build your website, branding, SEO, and online presence around the tools you already use.

How do you handle vaccination requirements in the booking flow?

Vaccination requirements can be handled through your existing pet care software and booking process. We can help set up the website flow so clients are guided to provide required vaccination details, upload records if needed, or complete required forms before appointments.

What if I already have a site on Wix, Squarespace, or PetExec's free site?

You can keep your existing website while upgrading your branding, SEO, and customer experience. We can either improve your current site or build a new conversion-focused website and help you transition smoothly without losing your online presence.

Do you do ongoing SEO or content writing after launch?

Yes. We offer ongoing SEO and content support after launch, including blog content, local SEO improvements, Google Business Profile optimization, and strategies to help your pet grooming business continue growing over time.

How do you handle multi-location pet care businesses?

We build scalable solutions for multi-location pet care businesses, including location-specific pages, local SEO strategies, Google Business Profile optimization, and content tailored to each service area to help every location attract local customers.

Can you surface reactive-dog or special-needs policies?

Yes. We can highlight reactive-dog, senior, and special-needs pet policies clearly on your website. We can create dedicated policy sections, intake questions, and booking guidance so pet parents understand requirements before scheduling an appointment.

What happens after the 30-day support window?

After the 30-day support window, your website remains yours and continues running normally. If you need ongoing updates, SEO, content, or additional support, we offer optional maintenance and growth services.

PetExec and Gingr are great for managing pet care operations, but our focus is different — we build a custom, conversion-focused website with branding, SEO, local search strategy, and content designed to attract new customers.
